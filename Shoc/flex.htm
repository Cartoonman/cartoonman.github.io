FLEX

General Information

FLEX is a high speed pager protocol developed by Motorola. Two Messaging Systems are currently defined, the FLEX one-way data messaging protocol and the ReFLEX two-way data messaging protocol.

Usage:

§ Broadcast paging over VHF.
