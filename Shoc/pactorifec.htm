PACTOR I FEC

General Information

Pactor-I mode is a proprietary standard developed by SCS GmbH & Co. KG, Hanau, Germany.        
The FEC/Unproto variant is used for broadcast transmissions.

Usage:

§ Broadcast data transmissions over HF.

§ During call set-up Pactor-I-FEC mode is used.
