LINK 11 CLEW

General Information

STANAG5511 CLEW mode is a NATO Standard for tactical data exchange. The Conventional Link Eleven Waveform (CLEW) is one of the modes defined within the Link 11 NATO Standard.

Usage:

§ Transfer of tactical data over HF and VHF.
