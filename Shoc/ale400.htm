ALE-400

General Information

ALE-400 is an Automatic Link Establishment system used by amateur radio. It is a version of ALE-2G adapted to the demands of amateur radio emergency traffic handling.

Usage:

§ Detection of the HF-channel best suited for data-transfer between two stations.
