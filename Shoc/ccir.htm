CCIR

General Information

These SelCal standards are based on the CCIR-Recommendations (now ITU) CCIR-1, CCIR-2(CCIR-7) and PCCIR. CCIR-1 and CCIR-2 vary in the nominal tone duration.

Usage:

§ Narrowband FM SelCal system in the VHF/UHF frequency range.
