ALE-3G

General Information

Automatic Link Establishment third generation is based on the standard MIL-STD 188-141B App.C, also known as STANAG 4538. ALE-3G is an improved version of ALE-2G, it is tailored to the needs of modern tactical communications systems. This system is used to detect and assign the HF-channel which is considered most reliable for data-communication at the given time.

Usage:

§ Detection of the HF-channel best suited for data-transfer between two stations or broadcast transmissions.
