DSC

General Information

DSC (Digital Selective Calling) is part of the GMDSS (Global Maritime Distress and Safety System). It provides automatically formatted distress alerts, urgency, safety and routine radio-telephone calls.

Usage:

§ Data communication over HF / VHF.
