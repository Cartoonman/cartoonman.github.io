Baudot Sync

General Information

The synchronous Baudot mode is a means to transfer printable characters over a communication channel. Synchronization in this case is achieved by using a fixed character-length and a combination of Start- and Stop-Bit of reverse polarity.

Usage:

§ Transfer of textual information.
