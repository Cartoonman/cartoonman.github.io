CIS-14

General Information

CIS-14 is a synchronous duplex teleprinter system with ARQ.    
This modem system is also known as AMOR and AMOR96.

Usage:

§ Data communication over HF.

§ Point-to-point communication between stations in CIS (Commonwealth of Independent States)-region.
