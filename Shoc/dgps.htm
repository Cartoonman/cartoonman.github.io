DGPS

General Information

DGPS is a radio standard for transmission of corrections to the satellite ranging measurements (GPS and GLONASS). This radio standard is based on the recommendations of the RTCM Special Committee 104 (SC-104).

Usage:

§ Transmission of differential correction signals.
