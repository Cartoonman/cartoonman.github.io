SAT-AERO-C

The SAT-AERO-C mode decodes the circuit switched traffic channels. There are two bit-rates: 8400 bps and 21000 bps.

In the 8400 bps mode only the raw AMBE data is decoded and saved. Speech decoding is not supported.

In the 21000 bps mode, speech is decoded and saved into .wav files. Live voice is output to the speaker at the same time. The data files are saved into the DATA-OUTPUT folder.
