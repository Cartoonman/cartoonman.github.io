CIS-12

General Information

CIS-12 is a Soviet military multi-channel modem. It features scrambled voice- or data-communication at a maximum data rate of 4800 bits/sec.       
This modem system is also known as MS5 and FIRE.

Usage:

§ Data communication over HF.
