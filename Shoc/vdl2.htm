VDL 2

General Information

The VHF Digital Link (VDL) Mode 2 is an ICAO standard developed by the Aeronautical Mobile Communications Panel (AMCP) providing data communication between aircraft and ground-based systems. Aeronautical VHF data links use the band 117.975 - 137 MHz assigned by the International Telecommunication Union.

Usage:

§ Data communication within the Aeronautical Telecommunication Network.
