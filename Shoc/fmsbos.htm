FMS-BOS

General Information

The radio reporting system, German “FunkMeldeSystem” (FMS), for agencies and organizations with safety assignments, German “Behörden und Organisationen mit Sicherheitsaufgaben” (BOS), is a radio communication system for security authorities and organizations.

Usage:

§ VHF security related communications.
