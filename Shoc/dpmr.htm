dPMR

General Information

digital Private Mobile Radio (dPMR) is a digital radio protocol for voice and data communications. dPMR is a narrowband (6.25 kHz channel spacing) FDMA based protocol described in the ETSI technical standards TS102 490 and TS102 658.

Usage:

§ Professional and private voice & data communications.
